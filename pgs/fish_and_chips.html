Fish and Chips

Description

Simple fish and chips made with cod fillets and twice-fried fries. Tastes great as-is or with malt vinegar, lemon, or tartar sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into strips
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 quarter vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 1/2 pounds cod fillets

Directions

  1. Make the chips by slicing and frying the potatoes
  2. Make the batter by mixing the flour, baking powder, seasonings, milk and egg
  3. Dredge the fish in batter
  4. Fry the fish until golden brown
  5. FRy the potatoes a second time, then serve alongside the fried fish
