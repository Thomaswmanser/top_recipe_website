Ramen

Description

This savory ramen soup is easy to make, ready in a flash, and very, very good! I like to add ginger, soy sauce, sesame and chili oil for a delicious flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili oil
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Directions

  1. Combine broth and noodles in a medium saucepan.
  2. Cover and bring to the boil over a high heat, stirring to break up the noodles
  3. Reduce the head to medium and add soy sauce, chilli oil and ginger.
  4. Simmer, uncovered for 10 minutes. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions
